Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is Rusonik Loans?
Rusonik Loans is a financial service provider offering quick and flexible loan options to individuals and businesses with easy application processes and affordable interest rates.
2. How do I apply for a loan?
You can apply for a loan directly on our website by filling out the loan application form and submitting the required documents.
3. What types of loans does Mara Loans offer?
We offer personal loans, business loans, and emergency loans, all designed to cater to different needs and purposes.
4. What are the requirements for applying for a loan?
To apply for a loan, you will need to provide identification documents, proof of income, and a valid bank account.
5. How long does it take to get approved?
Loan approval typically takes 24 to 48 hours, depending on the completeness of your application and the type of loan.
6. What is the interest rate on loans?
Our interest rates are competitive and vary depending on the loan type. You will be notified of the exact rate upon approval.
7. Can I repay my loan early?
Yes, you can repay your loan early without any penalties. Early repayment may even reduce the total interest paid.
8. How do I check the status of my loan application?
You can check the status of your loan application by logging into your account or contacting our customer support team.
9. How do I make loan repayments?
Repayments can be made via bank transfer, mobile money, or in-person at our branches. Detailed instructions will be provided upon approval.
10. Can I apply for a loan if I have bad credit?
Yes, we offer loans to individuals with less-than-perfect credit. Your application will still be considered based on other factors like income and repayment history.
11. Is there an age limit for loan applicants?
Applicants must be between the ages of 18 and 65 to qualify for a loan.
12. Can I apply for a loan if I am self-employed?
Yes, self-employed individuals are eligible for loans. You will need to provide proof of your business income.
13. What happens if I miss a loan repayment?
Missing a repayment may result in late fees and affect your credit score. It's important to inform us in case of any issues with payment.
14. Can I apply for a loan if I am not employed?
Yes, you qualify for a loan.
15. How is my loan eligibility determined?
Loan eligibility is determined by factors such as income, credit history, loan type, and repayment capacity.
16. How do I contact Mara Loans customer support?
You can contact customer support through email, phone, or live chat available on our website.
17. Are there any hidden fees associated with the loans?
There are no hidden fees. All fees and charges are disclosed during the loan application process.
18. Can I use my loan for any purpose?
Yes, our loans are flexible and can be used for a variety of purposes including personal expenses, business expansion, and more.
19. How do I apply for a top-up loan?
You can apply for a top-up loan after completing your initial loan repayment. The process is similar to applying for a new loan.
20. Is Rusonik Loans available outside my country?
Currently, we provide loans within Kenya, and we are working on expanding our services to other countries in the near future.
