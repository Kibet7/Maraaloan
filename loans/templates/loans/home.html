How to Apply

Step 1: Visit Our Website

Start by visiting our official website to get started with your loan application.

Step 2: Register and Pay the Registration Fee

Register on our platform and make the required registration fee payment to proceed.

Step 3: Log In

Log in to your account to begin the loan application process.

Step 4: Apply Now

Go to the 'Apply Now' section, fill in your personal details and provide information for two guarantors.

Step 5: Submit Your Application

Submit your completed loan application for processing.

Step 6: Wait for Processing (Under 24 Hours)

Our team will process your application, and it will be ready in less than 24 hours.

Step 7: Download Loan Agreement

Once approved, download your loan agreement from your account dashboard.

Step 8: Take Form to Employer for Verification and Stamping

Take the loan agreement to your employer for payment verification and stamping.

Step 9: Upload Signed Agreement

Upload the signed loan agreement to your account.

Step 10: Wait for Disbursement

After successful verification, your loan will be disbursed to your account.
